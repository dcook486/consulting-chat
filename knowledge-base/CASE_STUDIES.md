# Case Studies

## 1. Riverside Family Dental — Scheduling Automation

**Challenge:** Front desk staff spent 3+ hours daily answering phone calls for appointment scheduling. 15% no-show rate was costing the practice revenue.

**Solution:** AI-powered website with automated appointment booking, SMS reminders, and a chatbot handling common questions about insurance, procedures, and office hours.

**Results:**
- 60% reduction in scheduling-related phone calls
- No-show rate dropped from 15% to 6%
- 25 new patient inquiries captured per month through the chatbot
- Front desk staff redirected to higher-value patient interactions

**Timeline:** 3 weeks from kickoff to launch

## 2. Summit Physical Therapy — Lead Capture & Intake

**Challenge:** The practice was losing potential patients who visited the website after hours. Paper intake forms were slow and error-prone.

**Solution:** 24/7 AI chatbot for lead capture, digital patient intake forms, and automated follow-up emails for website visitors who didn't book.

**Results:**
- 40% increase in new patient bookings from website
- Patient intake time reduced from 15 minutes to 3 minutes
- 90% of new patients complete intake before arrival
- Staff saves 8+ hours per week on administrative tasks

**Timeline:** 2 weeks from kickoff to launch

---

## 3. Harmony Wellness Clinic — Multi-Provider Scheduling

**Challenge:** Clinic with 6 providers struggled with scheduling across multiple practitioners. Patients often called to ask which provider was available for their specific need.

**Solution:** Intelligent scheduling system that matches patients to the right provider based on their needs, with AI chatbot handling provider availability questions.

**Results:**
- 50% reduction in scheduling errors
- Patient satisfaction scores increased 30%
- 35% more efficient provider utilization
- After-hours booking now accounts for 20% of all appointments

**Timeline:** 4 weeks from kickoff to launch
