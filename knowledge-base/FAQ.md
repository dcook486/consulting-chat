# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What does Cook Systems Consulting do?
We build AI-powered websites for small businesses—especially medical and dental clinics—that automate scheduling, patient intake, and lead capture so your team can focus on patient care.

### How is this different from a regular website?
Our websites include an AI chatbot that can answer patient questions 24/7, book appointments automatically, and collect intake forms—all without your staff lifting a finger.

### Do I need technical knowledge to use this?
Not at all. We handle all the technical setup. Your team gets a simple dashboard and training session so everyone feels comfortable from day one.

## AI Chatbot

### What can the AI chatbot do?
- Answer common patient questions (hours, location, services, insurance)
- Book appointments directly into your calendar
- Collect patient intake information
- Qualify and capture new leads
- Provide after-hours support

### Will the chatbot give wrong medical advice?
The chatbot is designed to answer questions about your practice's services, scheduling, and policies—not provide medical advice. It will direct clinical questions to your staff.

### Can I customize what the chatbot says?
Absolutely. We train the chatbot on your specific services, policies, and FAQs. You can update the information anytime.

## Scheduling

### Which calendar systems do you integrate with?
Google Calendar, Outlook, Calendly, and most EHR/practice management systems. If you use something else, ask us—we can likely integrate it.

### Can patients book appointments without calling?
Yes! Patients can book directly through the website chatbot or scheduling widget, 24/7.
